Transforming homes and yachts since 1996

Three distinc divisions, one unique experience

AHT Residential specializes in providing custom enterteiment and home control systems that are simple to operate. Working closely with homeowners and their projects teams of interior designers, architects and builders, AHT Residential ensures that technology systems are flawlessly integrated, on time and on budget.

AHT Marine focuses on technology solutions for the modern yacht. Our experience working on same of the largest yachts in the world, combined with our propietary programming, monitoring and real time notification systems, ensures yacht owners and their crews experience the best and most reliable technology while at sea. With over 15 years of experience installing lighting, audio, video, enviroment systems, IT networks, comunications, navigation interfaces and security systems, AHT Marine can tackle highly complex projects, delivering the most advanced and comprehensive solutions available today.

AHT Commercial delivers complete building integration management for maximum energy and operational efficiency. Technology can be seamlessly integrated throughout a facility for central, global and remote systems management.
